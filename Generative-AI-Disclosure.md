# Generative AI Disclosure

For completing this team project, we used generative AI tools in a handful of ways. The details
below outline how the group used the technology to facilitate the project.

- **Content Generation** — ChatGPT was used to brainstorm ideas and structure the initial project
  outline, and to generate ideas for business problems and the types of visualizations best suited
  to each piece of data.
- **Research Assistance** — ChatGPT was used to quickly summarize the datasets so we could
  understand what we were working with, and to research the different types of alternative-energy
  vehicles.
- **Code Review and Debugging** — ChatGPT and Gemini were used to optimize code performance and to
  efficiently work through errors and keep the project moving.
- **Proofreading and Grammar Checks** — The built-in tools in Word and Google Docs were used to
  check spelling and grammar throughout the written deliverables.

Our team reviewed, edited, and validated all AI-generated content to ensure its accuracy, relevance,
and originality in accordance with academic integrity guidelines.
